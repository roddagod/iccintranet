Site Access

Only members and regular attenders of [[name|community]] are given access to this site. In order to gain access, church-goers must demonstrate a certain level of commitment to the church and to attending regularly. All visitors to the church must have had some contact with church staff before they are considered a regular attender and allowed to sign in.

Children Under Age 13

By default, no one under the age of 13 may sign into this site, nor is their information available anywhere on the site for other Users. You may, however, choose to display information about your children and allow them to sign in via the Privacy page. Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.

Messages

You have control over what can and cannot be done regarding messages from this site. If you wish to disable private messages altogether, you may do so by going to your Privacy page and unchecking the box labeled "Private Messages Enabled." Also, by default, every email address is hidden from others. This prevents people from sending you or your children email messages directly. Instant Messaging IDs are not allowed on profiles, either; this further protects children from being contacted without your knowledge.
